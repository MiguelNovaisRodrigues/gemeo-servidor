Your academic twin.
Live, connected, yours.

Gémeo Digital is a knowledge graph for researchers who also teach, create, and write. It connects your articles, courses, artistic projects, and deadlines in one visual space — and finds CFP opportunities while you sleep.

No spam. We'll write when it's ready.
◉
Visual knowledge graph
See how your writing, research, teaching, and artistic practice connect. Nodes cluster by domain, sized by depth of engagement.
⌖
CFP Radar
Proactive scanning of academic databases for calls for papers, conferences, and journals — scored for relevance to your specific profile.
⊕
Capture anything
One-click browser bookmarklet, paste from other AIs, sync from Google Calendar, Gmail, and institutional email. Your graph stays up to date.
⊞
Teaching management
Course programs, lesson plans, assessment briefs, and student tracking — all connected to your research graph, not siloed in a separate app.
◷
Deadline rings
Every node shows its deadline as a circular arc — growing from green to red as the date approaches. Urgency is visible at a glance.
⇌
Daily sync
Every morning, a briefing: what needs attention today, what CFPs are open, what's due this week — pulled from your calendars and email.

Built for a specific kind of person

You're a researcher. But you also teach. And you make things — photographs, installations, publications, instruments. Your work doesn't fit neatly into Notion or Obsidian. It lives across domains that are actually deeply connected.

Existing tools treat your teaching and your research as separate. They don't know that the paper you're writing feeds the course you're building, which feeds the artistic project you're developing. Gémeo does.

It was built by a photographer and doctoral researcher who needed exactly this. The graph isn't a metaphor — it's the actual structure of the work.

Who it's for

PhD researchers in arts & humanities
Juggling dissertation writing, conference submissions, and a teaching load. CFP Radar alone is worth the subscription.
Artist-academics
Your practice IS your research, but your institution doesn't see it that way. Gémeo connects both sides of your work in one graph.
Independent researchers & critics
No institutional support. CFP Radar finds the open calls, the graph keeps the ideas connected, the daily briefing keeps you on track.
